FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the questions we get most often

What areas do you serve in Jodhpur?

We provide pickup and delivery across all major areas of Jodhpur including Shastri Nagar, Ratanada, Paota, Chopasni, Sardarpura and surrounding localities.

How long does it take to clean clothes?

Regular wash & fold takes 24-48 hours. Dry cleaning typically takes 2-3 days. Express 4-hour service is available for urgent orders at additional charges.

Do you handle delicate fabrics like silk and wool?

Yes! We specialise in delicate fabrics including silk, wool, cashmere, and ethnic wear. Our expert team uses appropriate cleaning methods for each fabric type.

What if my clothes get damaged?

All garments are fully insured during our care. In the rare event of damage, we provide fair compensation based on the garment's value (up to ₹20,000 per order).

Do you offer same-day service?

Yes! Same-day service is available for urgent orders at 50% additional charge. Please call before 10 AM for same-day pickup and delivery.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash on delivery, all UPI apps (PhonePe, Google Pay, Paytm) and bank transfers. Payment is collected at the time of delivery.
