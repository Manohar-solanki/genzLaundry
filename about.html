The Full Story

How It All Began

Every great business starts with a personal frustration. Ours started with a pile of dirty laundry and zero time to deal with it.

In 2023, our founder — a young entrepreneur from Jodhpur — noticed a massive gap in the market. The city had no reliable, affordable, on-demand laundry service. Students living in hostels were struggling. Working professionals had no time. Families needed help with heavy textiles like blankets and curtains.

The solution was obvious: a professional laundry service that comes to you, cleans your clothes to hotel standards, and delivers them back fresh — all at prices that make sense.

GenZ Laundry was born. We started small — just a team of two, a washing machine, and a WhatsApp number. Word spread fast. Within months, we were serving hundreds of customers across Jodhpur. The reviews poured in. The orders multiplied. We upgraded our equipment, trained our team, and built our 10-step cleaning process to guarantee consistent quality every single time.

Today, we're proud to be Jodhpur's most loved laundry service — but we're just getting started.
